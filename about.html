Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting

At Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting, our mission is to help people experience the thrill, beauty, and peace of the river in a safe and memorable way. We believe in building confidence, deepening friendships, and creating stories you’ll talk about for years. Whether you are a first-time paddler or a seasoned rafter, we are committed to guiding you with professionalism, respect, and genuine enthusiasm for the outdoors.

History

Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting began as a small, family-run operation with a single raft and a big dream: to share our love of the river with others. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted outfitter, guiding thousands of guests down some of the most beautiful and exciting rivers in the region. Our guides are experienced, certified, and passionate about what they do, and many of them started out just like our guests— a little nervous at first, but eager for adventure. From day one, our focus has been on safety, stewardship of the environment, and genuine hospitality on and off the water.
